What's Umarket all about?

Umarket is a Nigeria-based online marketplace that enables buyers|sellers of goods|services carry out their business transactions seamlessly and easily.

With Umarket, "SELLERS" can easily host their online stores at an affordable rate and showcase their products|services with ease, while "BUYERS" can stress-less-ly search for business owners|sellers who can deliver their desired products|services from within their locality.

Whether you're a seller or a buyer, Umarket is here for you!

With a Umarket account, you can either sell|buy products|services just like the way you would in a physical marketplace; only VERY MUCH EASIER.

With a Umarket Seller account, you can:
  • Host an online store for your business of any kind.
  • Be able to save as many customer's contact as possible, depending on your subscription type.
  • Receive payments for delivered products|services securely on the platform.
With a Umarket BUYER account, you can:
  • Search for and contact relevant sellers who could deliver your desired product|service from within your locality.
  • Be able to save as many seller's contact as possible.
  • Pay for products|services securely on the platform.
